Chrome Hearts is a luxury brand known for its edgy, rock-inspired aesthetic that blends high-end fashion with biker and punk subcultures. The brand produces a wide range of items including: Premium leather clothing Sterling silver jewelry Sunglasses Furniture and home goods Denim and streetwear Its designs often feature gothic motifs like crosses, daggers, fleur-de-lis, and bold engravings, making it a favorite among celebrities, musicians, and fashion-forward individuals who appreciate craftsmanship and rebellious style. Chrome Hearts was founded in 1988 in Los Angeles by Richard Stark, a leather enthusiast, and John Bowman, a leather manufacturer. They began in a garage, frustrated by the lack of quality leather jackets that matched their tastes. Soon after, Leonard Kamhout, a skilled silversmith, joined the team 1. The brand gained early traction when it designed costumes for the 1989 cult film Chopper Chicks in Zombietown, which featured the girlfriend of Sex Pistols guitarist Steve Jones. Jones wore Chrome Hearts on stage, and soon other rock legends like Motley Crüe and Guns N’ Roses followed suit 1. By the early 1990s, Chrome Hearts had caught the attention of the fashion world. In 1992, it won the CFDA Accessory Designer of the Year Award, presented by Cher, further cementing its reputation 1. Despite its success, Stark has always resisted being labeled a traditional fashion brand. He famously said, “Chrome Hearts has got nothing to do with the fashion world. We don’t have any seasons. I make things when I wanna make them, because I wanna make them.” The brand expanded globally, especially in Japan, thanks to a collaboration with Rei Kawakubo of Comme des Garçons, who showcased Chrome Hearts in her Tokyo flagship store
